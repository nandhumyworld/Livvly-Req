## Research Methodology

### Search Strategy
1. **Query 1:** "India dating apps market size 2026 2027 revenue projections social apps market"
   - Focus: TAM validation (dating apps market)
   - Results: 10 sources, 5 high-quality sources used

2. **Query 2:** "India voice-based social apps market size live audio streaming apps revenue 2026"
   - Focus: SAM validation (voice-based social apps)
   - Results: 10 sources, 4 high-quality sources used

3. **Query 3:** "India regional language apps market Tamil Telugu Kannada Malayalam tier 2 tier 3 cities 2026"
   - Focus: SOM validation (regional language + tier-2/3 cities)
   - Results: 10 sources, 6 high-quality sources used

### Source Reliability Assessment

**High Reliability:**
- Established market research firms (Grand View Research, Statista, Market Research Future)
- Government publications (IndBiz Economic Diplomacy)
- Established tech news publications (Inc42, Storyboard18)

**Medium Reliability:**
- Newer market research firms (MarkNtel Advisors, Verified Market Reports)
- Industry blogs/agency publications (Atom Communications, Vxplore)
- Business analysis blogs (Rahul Malodia)

**Triangulation Approach:**
- Used multiple sources for each market sizing component (TAM, SAM, SOM)
- Cross-referenced findings across high and medium reliability sources
- Adopted conservative estimates when sources diverged

### Limitations

1. **India-Specific Data Scarcity:**
   - Global voice social apps data available ($30.7B), India-specific harder to isolate
   - Had to estimate India's share (~8-10% of global market)

2. **SOM Calculation Assumptions:**
   - No direct research on "voice-first regional language dating/social apps" market size
   - Had to synthesize from multiple data points (regional language adoption 68%, tier-2/3 growth 70%, voice-first preference)
   - SOM estimate (₹150 Cr) involves more assumptions than TAM/SAM

3. **Market Definition Ambiguity:**
   - "Dating apps" vs "Social apps" vs "Voice-based social" categories overlap
   - Careful to avoid double-counting between TAM and SAM
   - LIVVLY spans multiple categories (dating + social + voice + regional)

4. **Projection Uncertainty:**
   - All estimates are projections, not current actuals
   - Market dynamics can shift (regulatory changes, competitor innovations)
   - Conservative estimates used to account for uncertainty
