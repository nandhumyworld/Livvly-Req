#### 4. **04-market-segments.md**
Detailed market segmentation analysis

**User Segments** (5 primary):
1. **Urban Young Professionals** (15M TAM): High ARPU (₹500+), 40-50% conversion
2. **Tier-2 City Aspirants** (30M TAM): Regional language first, 20-30% conversion
3. **Regional Language Enthusiasts** (40M TAM): 50-60% conversion, high engagement
4. **Diaspora/NRI** (5M TAM): 70%+ conversion, ₹3000+/month ARPU
5. **Casual/Social** (50M TAM): 5-10% conversion, low ARPU

**Creator Segments** (3 types):
1. **Professional Content Creators**: 200-500 creators (Y1), 60-70% revenue
2. **Emerging Talents**: 8,500-9,500 creators (Y1), 30-40% revenue
3. **Part-Time Creators**: 500-1,000 creators (Y1), 10% revenue

**Geographic Segments**:
- Tamil Nadu (launch, 2.5K creators, Y1)
- Andhra Pradesh (Q2 2026, 2.5K creators, Y1)
- Karnataka (Q3 2026, 1K creators, Y1)
- Diaspora (Y2 focus, 50K-100K users)

**Key Takeaways**:
- Regional language enthusiasts = largest TAM (40M) + highest engagement
- Creator diversity (professional + emerging) needed for authentic platform
- Prioritize 3 user segments for Year 1 (P1); diaspora/casual (P2/P3)
