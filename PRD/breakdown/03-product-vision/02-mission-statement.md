## Mission Statement

> "Connect people through voice in their native language; provide creators with the highest revenue share in the industry; build safe, moderated spaces for genuine interactions."

---

## Mission Decomposed

### Mission Pillar 1: Voice-First Native Language Connection

**Statement:** "Connect people through voice in their native language"

#### What This Means
- **Voice as Primary Medium:** Audio/voice is the core interaction mode (not text-based like Tinder)
- **Native Language:** Users interact in Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, etc., not forced English
- **Emotional Connection:** Voice conveys emotion, tone, personality better than text or video

#### Implementation Commitments
- [ ] All UI/UX available in Tamil, Telugu, Kannada (minimum)
- [ ] Voice quality codec optimization for regional dialects
- [ ] Regional music/cultural sensitivity in marketing and product
- [ ] Voice-to-text transcription accurate for regional languages (>95%)

#### Target Users
- Users who prefer voice over text/video
- Non-English speakers uncomfortable with English-only platforms
- Users in regions with slower internet (voice more efficient than video)

---

### Mission Pillar 2: Creator Highest Revenue Share

**Statement:** "Provide creators with the highest revenue share in the industry"

#### What This Means
- **Industry-Leading:** 75-85% creator share, vs. 50-60% from competitors (FRND, Tango)
- **Sustainable Income:** Creators can earn meaningful livelihood from the platform
- **Competitive Advantage:** Attracts quality creators and drives user engagement

#### Financial Model
From ₹500 user spend:
- Google Play: 30% = ₹150
- LIVVLY Platform: 35% = ₹175
- **Creator (Gross): 35% = ₹175** (Standard model)
- OR **Creator (Gross): 65% = ₹325** (High-share model for top creators)

#### Implementation Commitments
- [ ] Creator payout system accurate to the rupee
- [ ] T+1 payout (next business day) vs. T+3 to T+7 from competitors
- [ ] Transparent earnings dashboard for all creators
- [ ] No hidden fees or deductions (except regulatory TDS)
- [ ] Creator analytics: Real-time earnings, gift source, call duration

---

### Mission Pillar 3: Safe, Moderated Spaces

**Statement:** "Build safe, moderated spaces for genuine interactions"

#### What This Means
- **KYC + Verification:** All users identity-verified to prevent catfishing/fraud
- **Moderation:** Active monitoring and removal of inappropriate content
- **Trust Building:** Users feel safe sharing personal info and building relationships
- **Genuine Interactions:** Prevent bots, scammers, predators

#### Safety Mechanisms Required
- [ ] Phone number + OTP verification (mandatory)
- [ ] Photo ID verification for high-trust features
- [ ] 24/7 audio content monitoring (AI + human moderation)
- [ ] User reporting system with <24hr response
- [ ] Automatic detection of explicit content, harassment, scams
- [ ] Account suspension/deletion for policy violations

#### Compliance Areas
- Regional language moderation (content reviewers fluent in Tamil/Telugu/Kannada)
- Child safety (age verification, no minors under 18)
- Harassment prevention (block, report, mute features)
- Data privacy compliance (GDPR-ready, India data residency)

## Operational Implications

### For Product Team
- Prioritize regional language features above English features
- Design voice experience to be intuitive, fast, reliable
- Build creator dashboard as prominently as user dashboard

### For Creator Relations
- Communicate payout model clearly and frequently
- Highlight "highest in industry" positioning
- Make earnings visible and transparent

### For Trust & Safety Team
- Over-invest in moderation initially (build trust from day 1)
- Make reporting easy and response visible
- Be transparent about policy enforcement

### For Finance
- Ensure payout engine is bulletproof (creator trust is critical)
- Track creator satisfaction with payout timing and accuracy
- Model sustainable economics with 35-40% platform share
