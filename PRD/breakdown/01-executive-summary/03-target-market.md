# Target Market - LIVVLY

## Geographic & Demographic Focus

### Primary Market
- **Age Range**: 18-35 years old
- **Regions**: Tamil Nadu, Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka
- **Urban Classification**: Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities
- **Language**: Tamil speakers (primary), Telugu and Kannada (future)

### Secondary Market
- **Indian Diaspora** seeking regional language connections
- **Non-resident Indians (NRIs)** wanting to stay connected with home communities

---

## User Personas

### Persona 1: Lonely Professional
- **Age**: 22-30
- **Occupation**: IT, BPO, or other service sector workers
- **Motivation**: Seek companionship after work hours
- **Usage Pattern**: Evening/night calls and room participation
- **Monetization**: Users (spenders), not creators

### Persona 2: Friendship Seekers
- **Age**: 18-35
- **Motivation**: Find platonic friends, expand social network
- **Usage Pattern**: Regular engagement, varied time zones
- **Monetization**: Mixed (both users and potential low-tier creators)

### Persona 3: Content Creators
- **Age**: 20-35
- **Background**: May have existing social media presence or looking to build one
- **Motivation**: Earn passive/active income through monetization
- **Usage Pattern**: Regular streaming, building audience
- **Monetization**: Creators earning through gifts and subscriptions
