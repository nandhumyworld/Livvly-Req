# Business Goals & Growth Targets - LIVVLY

## 6-Month Goals (Soft Launch Phase)
- **Active Users**: 50,000
- **Monthly Gross Merchandise Value (GMV)**: ₹20 Lakhs (₹20,00,000)
- **Key Focus**: Market validation in Tamil Nadu, creator recruitment, user acquisition

## 12-Month Goals (Growth Phase)
- **Active Users**: 2,00,000 (4x growth from 6-month)
- **Monthly GMV**: ₹1 Crore (₹1,00,00,000)
- **Key Focus**: Regional expansion (Telugu, Kannada), monetization optimization, platform stability

## 24-Month Goals (Scale Phase)
- **Active Users**: 10,00,000 (5x growth from 12-month)
- **Monthly GMV**: ₹5 Crore (₹5,00,00,000)
- **Key Focus**: Multi-regional dominance, creator ecosystem maturity, profitability path

---

## Key Metrics Derived from Goals

### User Acquisition Metrics
- **6-month**: ~8,330 users/month
- **12-month**: ~12,500 users/month (accelerating)
- **24-month**: ~37,500 users/month (scale phase)

### Monetization Metrics
- **6-month ARPU** (Avg. Revenue Per User): ₹400 (~$5)
- **12-month ARPU**: ₹500 (~$6)
- **24-month ARPU**: ₹500 (~$6) - monetization plateau expected

### GMV Growth Rate
- **6-to-12 months**: 5x growth
- **12-to-24 months**: 5x growth (consistent scaling)

## Critical Questions for Goal Validation

1. **Seasonality**: Do these GMV targets account for seasonal variations (festival seasons, university academic calendars)?
2. **User Retention Targets**: What's the expected DAU/MAU ratio at each phase?
3. **Creator Supply**: How many active creators needed to support these user numbers?
4. **Geographic Sequencing**: Is the 6-month goal Tamil Nadu only, or multi-region?
5. **Break-even Timeline**: When does the product aim for profitability?

## Success Metrics & KPIs

### User Metrics
- DAU (Daily Active Users)
- MAU (Monthly Active Users)
- Retention: Day-7, Day-30, Day-90
- Churn rate (target: <5% monthly)

### Creator Metrics
- Number of active creators
- Creator earnings (average, median, top-10%)
- Creator retention rate

### Business Metrics
- GMV (Gross Merchandise Value)
- Revenue (platform take)
- ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)
- Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- Lifetime Value (LTV)
- LTV:CAC Ratio (target: 3:1 or higher)
