# Product Overview - LIVVLY

## Core Identity

**Product Name:** LIVVLY
**Category:** Voice-First Social & Dating Application
**Primary Market:** Indian Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities

## Product Description

LIVVLY is a voice-first social and dating application that enables users to connect through multiple channels:

- **Voice & Video Calls** - Direct peer-to-peer communication
- **Live Audio Rooms** - Group voice conversations (like Clubhouse/Spaces model)
- **Virtual Gifting** - Monetization through digital gifts during calls/streams
- **AI Companion** - AI-powered interactions alongside real creators

## Core Value Proposition

Users can connect, socialize, and monetize their engagement through voice, with a focus on regional language support and creator-friendly payouts.
